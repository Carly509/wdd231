Community Garden Initiative

Site Plan Document

1. Site Name

GrowTogether - This name was selected because it represents both the literal growing of plants and the metaphorical growth of community connections through gardening. It embodies the collaborative nature of community gardening while being memorable and positive.

Optional domain availability: growtogether.org

2. Site Purpose

The GrowTogether website serves as a central hub for the Community Garden Initiative. Its purpose is to inform community members about sustainable gardening practices, coordinate volunteer efforts, facilitate knowledge sharing, and build a network of local gardeners. The site provides educational resources, event information, and community engagement opportunities to promote urban agriculture and environmental stewardship.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I'm new to gardening and want to start growing vegetables in my backyard. What resources are available to help me get started?"

Scenario 2: "I'm interested in joining a community garden plot. How do I sign up and what are the membership requirements?"

Scenario 3: "When is the next community seed-sharing event, and how can I participate or volunteer?"

  • Secondary Color (Orange): Call-to-action buttons, highlighted content, and important notices

  • Text Color: Body text and content

5. Typography

The typography selections provide good readability while maintaining a clean, modern look that resonates with environmental themes.

Montserrat

This font will be used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to provide a clean, modern look with good readability.

Example of Montserrat Bold for emphasis

Open Sans

This font will be used for body text and general content. It offers excellent readability at various sizes and works well for both digital and print.

7. Content Structure

Based on the project proposal, the website will consist of the following pages:

Home Page:

  • Introduction to the Community Garden Initiative
  • Highlight upcoming projects and volunteer opportunities
  • Featured events and news
  • Quick links to resources and registration

About Us:

  • Mission statement and goals
  • History of the initiative
  • Profiles of founders and key volunteers
  • Community impact statistics

Get Involved:

  • Membership information
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Donation options
  • Contact form for inquiries
